Shutdown Consequences

During a government shutdown, active-duty service members do not get paid‚Äîeven though they must report for duty. This includes all branches, National Guard, and deployed personnel. Civilian support staff are furloughed unless deemed essential.

This pay freeze has happened repeatedly: in 2013, 2018‚Äì2019, and now again in 2025. The longest shutdown lasted 35 days under President Trump in 2019. Each time, troops worked unpaid until Congress passed a funding bill.
