The Earlier You Start
Starting at 25 with $100/month can grow to $250K by retirement. Wait until 50, and you’ll need $500/month to reach the same goal. At 60, it’s $1,000/month. Time is the multiplier.

• $25/month → ~$63K by retirement
• $50/month → ~$125K
• $100/month → ~$250K
These estimates assume a 7% annual return over 40 years.
Is Social Security Enough?
Social Security was never designed to be a full retirement fund. Most retirees need at least two other sources of income—savings, pensions, or investments. If the system falters, it won’t just be a policy failure. It’ll be a national reckoning.
