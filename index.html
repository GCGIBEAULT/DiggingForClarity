Why Digging For Clarity

A new phenomena is taking over the country. We all see it. We pretend it's not real. But, my opinion, it's very real. Political division that is destroying relationships. It eats at your heart to think it. I would never do that you might say. Well, I did. We no longer care what other people think unless we think alike. Anything else means the other side is just stupid. I did a little reassessment and discovered that I knew a lot of "stupid" people. Not a lot. But far too many. And it has nothing to do with our crushing debt or the many wars we're throwing our tax dollars at around the world. It has nothing to do with politics at all. Love Trump. Or hate Trump. You, are stupid. Either way. These are playground politics and I was ashamed of myself. I have my opinions of how we got there that mostly involve the media, print and live. They report news, some of it very good. But what they all have in common is that they have taken a stance; chosen a side. Many of their stories are written to induce anger. And anger drives ratings. They put that hook in and the next day you're tuning in and getting another hook. Watch us, they're all screaming. At first I thought it was only the liberal media doing this. I took a closer look and guess what? They "all" do it. News equals viewership equals money. And like it or not, the news is a very big business. The "hook" is what keeps us tuning in and wanting us to come back for more. So I wrote Digging For Clarity. I wrote it keeping you in mind but mostly I wrote it for myself. Along the journey, I only watched snippets of local news. Zero national news. That is where the fishing line and hook come in the hardest. I started reading news again after a very long time off from it. I wasn't reading the news the same way though. I was hunting for stories to put on DFC that would keep me informed but not enraged or taking sides on an issue. I have a side. Of course. Everyone does. I wanted to know what the big, fat, beautiful bill was. I wanted to know the good. Who it helps. And I wanted to know who it hurts. And by how much. What good comes from hating the person that signed it into law and the Congress that gave it to him to sign. That's not education. That's emotion. My goal on this page is to erase emotion from the news. One of the favorite sayings of our time, "it is what it is". I'm not healed from this divisional cord yet, but I am making positive progress. Maybe I'll never be cured but, I am aware of my weaknesses and I'm trying to fix them. I want to be like a Marine, the best I can be. My life is a journey, like yours is. And like all journeys, there are obstacles along the way. How you deal with them is what makes you who you are. I wish you all a wonderful journey and hope this page might help you, even if just a little bit.
