Install Digging For Clarity

Get instant access from your home screen—no tracking, no drama.

Detecting your device...

Laptop/Desktop

  • Chrome or Edge: Click the install icon in the address bar

Android Phones & Tablets

  • Tap the three-dot menu in Chrome or Edge
  • Select “Add to Home screen”
  • Confirm the name and tap “Add”

iPhones & iPads

  • Tap the Share icon in Safari (square with arrow)
  • Scroll down and tap “Add to Home Screen”
  • Confirm the name and tap “Add”
